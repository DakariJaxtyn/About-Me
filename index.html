Art

I have always enjoyed looking at art, especially people that are drawn in unique ways, and I practice drawing often so I can achieve an art style that impresses myself. At the age of 10 I was still using dots as eyes and circles as noses but practising constantly has allowed me to improve at a steady pace. I started drawing with the intention to become good at art at the beginning of lockdown when I had nothing else to do. In the time period between the beginning of lockdown and when I am writing this, my art has gotten rapidly better. I am now happy with how I can shade, draw side angles, eyes and hair. However I want to improve at drawing people full-body and different poses and realstic clothing. I enjoy to draw fanart of characters and celebrities and viewing other's fanart on instagram and pinterest.
